LUCA IMMERSIVE · Character Design

Meet LUCA.

The face of The Field. A guide given form, designed from every angle.

LUCA is the character at the heart of the LUCA IMMERSIVE world. In the story, everything imaginable lives in a place called The Field, a white space where ideas first take shape.

LUCA is that field's light, given a form. A guide and a translator, not a creator. He reveals what is already there and hands it to the people in the room. The face of a shared, infinite source of imagination.
